IOR / EOR Services

We Take Legal Ownership. So You Don't Have To.

Whether you're moving protein, grains, chemicals, machinery, or any other regulated commodity across borders — Pyrocor serves as your Importer or Exporter of Record. Our IOR and EOR capability is fully commodity-agnostic, built on deep regulatory expertise across 80+ jurisdictions.

Service 01

Importer of Record (IOR)

We act as the legally responsible importing entity in the destination country — managing customs entry, duties, taxes, licensing, and regulatory filings on your behalf, across any commodity class. This allows you to deliver goods into markets where you hold no local legal presence, without establishing a registered entity.

  • Full customs clearance and documentation management
  • Duty and tax calculation, payment and reconciliation
  • Import permits and licensing across 80+ markets
  • Commodity-specific certification coordination (veterinary, phytosanitary, SABS, etc.)
  • In-country compliance and regulatory liaison
  • Risk absorption and full legal liability management
Service 02

Exporter of Record (EOR)

Where the original seller lacks export authority, trade compliance standing, or regulatory registration, Pyrocor assumes the role of Exporter of Record — managing the full export compliance cycle for any commodity, from any origin, into any destination.

  • Export licensing and authorisation management
  • Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, health and conformity certificates
  • Commodity-specific export documentation (phytosanitary, CITES, dual-use, etc.)
  • Sanctions screening and trade compliance checks
  • Letter of Credit and documentary credit handling
  • Multi-jurisdictional and multi-commodity export coordination
